Our Story

Dear our beloved plant lover,

I'm Lucy - The young CEO of Duspro.

I was born and raised in a small rural area in the North of Vietnam, so my family grows a lot of trees. Duspro is my small family-owned business with 11 members including me, my parents, sister, aunt, uncle, and 5 female neighbors. We provide indoor plant care products.

About me, I usually spend an hour every morning chatting and taking care of all plants in my house before starting a new day. That time's like a dose of "doping" for me to kick off a productive day.

Thanks to that routine I realized my plants can become much more alive when I give them powerful tools. Then many different ideas kept popping up in my head.

Until now, after nearly two years with Duspro, I'm so happy and proud to provide some powerful & useful products for indoor plant care to my dear customers. It is not only my effort but also the contribution of my whole family behind.

Under the motto "Treat plants as your friend", all of our products are handmade with heart and undergo rigorous testing, so you can rest assured about quality and safety when using our products.

Hope that you will be satisfied with our products and service.
Wish you a fresh day!
Sincerely, Lucy with love.

We are committed to bringing you 100% handmade indoor plant care products with environmentally friendly materials, ensuring the health of plants and consumers.
